## Review Rules

- Use a formal paper, official technical page, or public project as the primary
  source; a social post alone is not enough.
- Record what is missing rather than restating an unverified claim.
- Update the last-checked date whenever the source is reviewed.
- Remove candidates that remain unsupported, repeat marketing claims, are
  disproven, or no longer have a meaningful game-development connection.
- When promoting an item, add the bounded entry to `README.md` and record the
  change in `UPDATES.md`.
